# **App Name**: Adeeya Ethos

## Core Features:

- Vision Catalyst AI Tool: A generative AI tool that uses economic principles and servant leadership philosophy to provide tailored business strategy and leadership insights for young entrepreneurs.
- Venture Nexus: An interactive, multi-dimensional display showcasing the interconnectivity of fintech, energy, and consultancy sectors within the Adeeya portfolio.
- Ministry & Impact Map: A visual timeline and geographical interface representing social impact work across RJM Church Kikuyu and regional board leadership.
- Leadership Metrics Dashboard: Live visualization of real-world impact data, including years of leadership, global positioning, and venture growth stats.
- Thoughtweaver AI Tool: A tool that synthesizes weekly economic notes and theological reflections into actionable daily mission statements.
- Encrypted Global Outreach: A streamlined communication interface allowing potential partners from Nairobi and beyond to reach out via authenticated contact channels.
- The Pillar Library: A modular UI component featuring insights into The Economist, The Entrepreneur, and The Servant leadership styles.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Deep Noble Gold (#cfac4c), reflecting executive leadership, economic prosperity, and the light of faith.
- Background Color: Obsidian Onyx (#15130f), a deeply desaturated warm hue providing a prestigious and cinematic dark environment.
- Accent Color: Clay Terra-cotta (#a64f40), an analogous earthy tone suggesting groundness and human-centric service.
- Headline Font: 'Playfair Display' (serif) for a sophisticated, classic, and high-end literary feel. Body Font: 'DM Sans' (sans-serif) for contemporary precision and clarity. Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- An expansive grid-based layout inspired by modern gallery aesthetics, using generous whitespace (or 'dark-space') and asymmetrical elements to denote vision and movement.
- Thin-line golden icons with soft gradients that represent complexity in economics (graphs) and simplicity in faith (serene shapes).
- Staggered 'Fade Up' entrance animations for page components, creating a feeling of purpose and unfolding revelation.
